Welcome to NetProgress

NetProgress provides an easy way to monitor the progress of your long running programs from anywhere.

Using a simple to use library and intuitive dashboard, you can see exactly how much progress your project has made without having to log in through cumbersome remote tools like SSH, while also avoiding the risk of accidentally closing your running application.
